Naturally sustainable wool

A sustainable, renewable and biodegradable natural fibre, with some great performance characteristics. It also provides and retains warmth while being naturally flame retardant. We believe that wool is hard to beat.

Production & Sustainability

Our commitment to maintaining strong connections with our suppliers lies at the heart of our business. We emphasize personal visits to the factories, ensuring a close relationship and a deep understanding of our production processes

100% Organic Cotton

Our cotton is grown on soil that is free of toxic pesticides and fertilizers. The fabric is dyed and finished without the use of heavy metals or allergenic disperse dyes.

Sustainabilty

Organic Cotton

Our dedication to organic cotton reflects our commitment to ethical and responsible sourcing, supporting farms that prioritize sustainability and fair labor practices.

Sustainabilty

RWS Wool

We prioritize RWS (Responsible Wool Standard) certified wool in most of our products. RWS ensures humane treatment of sheep, promoting their well-being. RWS farms follow sustainable practices, reducing the environmental impact.

Sustainabilty

Vegetable Tanned Leather

We place a strong emphasis on using vegetable-tanned leather for all of our leather products. Vegetable-tanned leather is an eco-friendly choice. It's processed using natural plant-based tannins, reducing the use of harmful chemicals and minimizing the environmental impact.
