# Phase 4 PDP Host And Behavior Reopen Evidence Risks v1

1. `Inference`: PDP reopen could overreach into full commerce behavior too quickly.
2. `Inference`: Product form, variant handling, media behavior, recommendation, and reassurance work could collapse into one unstable lane.
3. `Inference`: AJAX cart or cart drawer behavior would increase runtime and commerce risk beyond the evidence package.
4. `Verified`: The mitigation is to admit only a docs-only runtime-boundary package and explicitly block direct implementation until that boundary is authored and verified.
5. `Verified`: The mitigation also keeps `PDP companion`, recommendations, complementary products, subscriptions, pickup/inventory complexity, search, preset, and hardening work fail-closed.
