- `Inference`: Permit a larger merchandising package for rails, recommendation behavior, or heavier commerce actions.

- `Inference`: This is conservative, but it would postpone unresolved family-level planning work and force presets to depend on incomplete higher-order surface decisions.

## Recommendation

- `Verified`: **Recommend Option A.**
- `Inference`: The next honest controlled expansion should formalize a shoppable-media planning boundary, because it is the most structurally distinct unresolved homepage-first family and clearly requires a larger interaction-aware planning step beyond the bounded merchandising protocol.
